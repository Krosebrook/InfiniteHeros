# User Guide

Welcome to Infinite Heroes (Flash Comics)! This guide will help you create your own AI-generated comic book adventure.

## 1. Getting Started
When you first open the app, you will be prompted to enter your Google Gemini API key. This key is required to generate the story and images. It is stored securely in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

## 2. Setting Up Your Story
On the main screen, you can configure your adventure:
- **Characters:** Upload images for your Hero, Sidekick (optional), and Villain (optional). The AI will analyze the images and generate names and backstories. Alternatively, click "Auto-Generate" to let the AI create them for you.
- **Genre & Art Style:** Choose from various genres (e.g., Superhero Action, Dark Sci-Fi) and matching art styles (e.g., Noir, Watercolor).
- **Language & Tone:** Select your preferred language and the overall tone of the story (e.g., Serious, Humorous).
- **Layout:** Choose how the comic panels will be arranged on the page.

## 3. Playing the Adventure
Once you click "Start Adventure," the AI will generate the first page of your comic.
- **Reading:** Read the narrative text and view the generated image. You can click the speaker icon to have the text read aloud.
- **Choices:** At the bottom of each panel, you will be presented with choices on how to proceed. Select an option to generate the next part of the story.
- **Dice Rolls:** Some choices require a dice roll (e.g., D20). Click the dice icon to roll and see if your action succeeds.
- **Character Chat:** Click on a character's portrait to open a chat window and interact with them directly.

## 4. Managing Your Journey
- **Inventory & Status:** View your current health, status effects, and collected items in the sidebar.
- **Multiverse Map:** Click the map icon to view a branching tree of your decisions. You can click on previous nodes to "time travel" and make different choices.
- **Export:** Click the export icon to save your comic as a PDF or ZIP file.
